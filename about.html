WHAT WE DO

Hey there!

We are a group of female-identifying, and genderqueer
folks who get together for regular social rides and are
involved in helping make Portland friendlier and safer
for cyclists. We recognize that women, people of color,
seniors, children, and low-income folks are the most
adversely affected by inequities in Portland's transportation
infastructure. That’s why we are working together to bring
about meaningful change for Portland’s most vulnerable
road users through activism, advocacy, and volunteer events.

Join us for our next event!
