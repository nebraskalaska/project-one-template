Check out what we've got going on!


All rides are casual, free, and appropriate for all skill and comfort levels unless otherwise indicated.
Questions about a particular event? Drop us a line!

Saturday | December 15 6pm - 10pm

Holiday Lights Night Ride

Come ride with us around Portland and take a look at all the pretty twinkly lights and holiday decorations. We'll have planned pit stops along the way to warm up with soups, stews, warm cocoa, and hot toddies

Location

Meet on the NW corner of Colonel Summers Park

Saturday | January 4
11am - 2pm

The New Year Ride

It's a New Year and a new LLC! To kick of the New Year we'll be hosting a casual weekend brunch ride around the city. We'll get a late start and stop for bite at a popular Portland brunch institution. The restaurant is able to accomodate vegan, gluten free, and most common allergies. If you have additional dietary concerns drop us a line and we'll get back to you ASAP!

Location

Meet at the SE Clinton and SE 12th

January 20 | 10am - 12pm

MLK Day of Service

Join us on Martin Luther King Junior Day to help spruce up the Eastbank Esplanade. We will be picking up garbage and handing out meals and toiletries to anyone in need. We will provide necessary supplies including rubber gloves and garbage bags. We will ride to a nearby cafe for coffee afterward.

Location

Meet under the Tillkum Bridge on the Eastbank Esplanade
